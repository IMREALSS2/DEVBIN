The Story of &#x44;&#x65;&#x76;&#x42;&#x69;&#x6E;

&#x48;&#x6F;&#x77;&#x20;&#x69;&#x74;&#x20;&#x73;&#x74;&#x61;&#x72;&#x74;&#x65;&#x64;&#x2E;&#x20;&#x48;&#x6F;&#x77;&#x20;&#x69;&#x74;&#x20;&#x65;&#x6E;&#x64;&#x65;&#x64;&#x2E;&#x20;&#x41;&#x6C;&#x6C;&#x20;&#x6F;&#x66;&#x20;&#x69;&#x74;&#x2E;

&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x31;
&#x5A;&#x65;&#x74;&#x61;&#x20;&#x53;&#x63;&#x72;&#x69;&#x70;&#x74;&#x73;
Before DevBin there was Zeta. Zeta Scripts. It was a Discord server that did a bit of everything — Discord tools, moomoo.io scripts, devast.io scripts. Nothing crazy at first, just a server with some scripts and a small group of people who were into it.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x32;
&#x5A;&#x65;&#x74;&#x61;&#x20;&#x62;&#x6C;&#x65;&#x77;&#x20;&#x75;&#x70;
Then Zeta took off. Like actually took off. The server was growing stupid fast, people were joining every day, scripts were getting passed around, and money was coming in. Zeta was doing numbers. It felt like it couldn't lose.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x33;
&#x45;&#x76;&#x65;&#x72;&#x79;&#x74;&#x68;&#x69;&#x6E;&#x67;&#x20;&#x66;&#x65;&#x6C;&#x6C;&#x20;&#x61;&#x70;&#x61;&#x72;&#x74;
But it did. Zeta started catching allegations left and right. Discord hit the server with violations. The whole thing started crumbling. That fast growth turned into a fast fall. The server that was on top was now fighting to stay alive, and it was losing.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x34;
&#x53;&#x53;&#x32;&#x20;&#x74;&#x61;&#x6B;&#x65;&#x73;&#x20;&#x6F;&#x76;&#x65;&#x72;
Zeta stepped down. Handed the keys to SS2. First thing SS2 did was kill the Zeta name and rebrand the whole thing into DevBin. New name, clean slate. DevBin was never going to be Zeta — it was never going to grow that fast or be that loud. But that wasn't the point. For SS2, DevBin was a learning experience. Servers, bots, scripting, all of it. He was there to get better.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x35;
&#x53;&#x53;&#x32;&#x20;&#x61;&#x6E;&#x64;&#x20;&#x74;&#x68;&#x65;&#x20;&#x68;&#x61;&#x74;&#x65;
SS2 caught his own wave of problems. Allegations came at him too. Multiple communities considered him one of the most hated people around. That's not an exaggeration — people genuinely did not like him. But SS2 didn't care. He kept running DevBin, kept scripting, kept doing his thing regardless of what people said.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x36;
&#x54;&#x68;&#x65;&#x20;&#x70;&#x6C;&#x75;&#x67;&#x20;&#x67;&#x65;&#x74;&#x73;&#x20;&#x70;&#x75;&#x6C;&#x6C;&#x65;&#x64;
DevBin was actually starting to grow again when SS2 shut it down. Not because it was failing. Not because Discord came after it. He just lost motivation. That's it. He wanted to move on to something else, something bigger. DevBin had already served its purpose — he learned what he needed to learn and was done with it.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x37;
&#x57;&#x68;&#x65;&#x72;&#x65;&#x20;&#x53;&#x53;&#x32;&#x20;&#x69;&#x73;&#x20;&#x6E;&#x6F;&#x77;
SS2 runs a completely different server now. Nothing to do with scripting. And he's doing well with it — actually successful. He still drops scripting videos on YouTube every now and then, so the old side of him isn't completely gone. People talk about whether he'll ever come back to scripting for real. Nobody knows. But knowing SS2, don't count it out.
&#x43;&#x68;&#x61;&#x70;&#x74;&#x65;&#x72;&#x20;&#x38;
&#x54;&#x68;&#x65;&#x20;&#x6C;&#x65;&#x67;&#x61;&#x63;&#x79;
Say what you want about SS2 but he had one of the craziest runs in the .io scripting scene. The speed Zeta grew at, the confidence he walked around with, the drama he stirred up in every community he touched — people still talk about it. From Zeta to DevBin, from hated to successful, the whole thing was a ride. And this page is the only proof it ever happened.
DevBin is dead. But if you know SS2, you know this probably isn't the end of the story.
